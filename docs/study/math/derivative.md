## 导数严格定义
$$
f'(x)=\lim _{\Delta x\rightarrow 0} \frac{f(x+\Delta x)-f(x)}{\Delta x}
$$

## 导数常用公式 & 结果
$$
\begin{array}{l}
C'=0\text{（$C$ 为常数）} \\
(x^n)'= n\cdot x^{n-1} \\
(a^x)'=a^x\ln a \\
(e^x)'=e^x \\
\sin(x)'=cos(x) \\
\ln(x)'=\frac 1 x \\
f(g(x))'=f'(g(x))\cdot g'(x)
\end{array}
$$

## 导数四则运算
$$
\begin{array}{l}
(u\pm v)'=u'\pm v' \\
(uv)'=u'\cdot v+u\cdot v' \\
\left(\frac{u}{v}\right)'=\frac{u'\cdot v-u\cdot v'}{v^2}
\end{array}
$$
